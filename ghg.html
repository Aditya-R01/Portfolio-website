AI-Powered Supply Chain Emission Factor Prediction Web App

The Supply Chain Emission Factors with Margins Predictor is a machine learning-powered web application.This project develops a predictive model to estimate emission factors within supply chains, a crucial step for quantifying environmental impact.

  • Develops a predictive model for emission factors in supply chains.
  • Aims to accurately quantify the environmental impact of supply chain activities.
  • Utilizes XGBoost, a robust machine learning algorithm, for prediction.
  • Identifies emission hotspots and facilitates data-driven decisions for reduction.
  • Supports the achievement of sustainability goals and responsible supply chain management.
