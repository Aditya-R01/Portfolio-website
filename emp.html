The Employee Salary Predictor is a machine learning-powered web application designed to estimate realistic salary figures for various job roles, education levels, and work locations. Utilizing a Random Forest model trained on authentic employee data, this tool empowers HR personnel and job seekers to make informed salary decisions based on market standards.

  • Predicts employee salaries based on education, experience, job title, location, age, and gender.
  • Intuitive user interface built with Streamlit for fast and easy input of employee details.
  • Interactive results: shows estimated monthly salary after prediction.
  • Employs robust Random Forest machine learning for high accuracy.
  • Ideal for HR professionals, candidates, and data science learning projects.
