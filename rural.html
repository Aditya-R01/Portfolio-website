Intelligent Classification of Rural Infrastructure Projects

💡 AI-Powered Smart Infrastructure Classification Tool

The Intelligent Classification of Rural Infrastructure Projects is a machine learning-driven solution designed to automatically categorize rural development projects under the PMGSY (Pradhan Mantri Gram Sadak Yojana) scheme. Leveraging AI and cloud-based deployment, the tool helps policymakers, engineers, and administrators efficiently classify infrastructure initiatives, ensuring better planning and execution.

  • Classifies rural infrastructure projects into predefined categories using AI/ML models.
  • Trained on structured project datasets to ensure reliable classification.
  • Deployed on IBM Cloud with AutoAI for automated model training and optimization.
  • Provides fast and accurate classification results through an easy-to-use web interface.
  • Enhances decision-making for government bodies and supports smart rural development initiatives.
  • Ideal for policy researchers, government agencies, and data science enthusiasts.
